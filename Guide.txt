Baccarat is a simple card game often found in casinos where players bet on which hand will win: the Player, the Banker, or if it will be a Tie.  
🔹 How it works: • Two hands are dealt: Player and Banker  
    • Each hand gets 2–3 cards • The goal is to get a total closest to 9  
🔹 Card values: • Cards 2–9 = face value • 10, J, Q, K = 0 • Ace = 1  
    • If the total is above 9, only the last digit counts (e.g., 15 = 5)  
🔹 Gameplay: • You don’t control the cards—you only place bets  
    • The dealer follows fixed rules to draw a third card if needed  
🔹 Betting options: • Player bet → wins if Player hand is higher  
    • Banker bet → wins if Banker hand is higher (most common)  
    • Tie bet → wins if both hands are equal (higher payout but risky)

Mines
Some tiles hide mines 💣, while others are safe 💎.

Goal:
🔹 Pick safe tiles without hitting a mine
🔹 The more safe tiles you uncover, the higher your winnings

🔹 How to Play
	1.	Place a Bet
	•	Choose how much money/chips you want to risk.
	2.	Select Number of Mines
	•	Example: 3 mines, 5 mines, etc.
	•	More mines = higher risk + higher reward
	3.	Start Clicking Tiles
	•	Each click reveals either:
	•	💎 Safe → you earn more
	•	💣 Mine → you lose your bet instantly
	4.	Cash Out Anytime
	•	You can stop anytime and take your current winnings
	•	If you keep going, the multiplier increases

🔹 Multiplier System
	•	Each safe tile increases your payout multiplier
	•	Example:
	•	1 safe tile → 1.2x
	•	3 safe tiles → 2.5x
	•	5 safe tiles → 10x+

🔹 Risk vs Reward
	•	Few mines → safer, lower profit
	•	Many mines → risky, huge profit potential

The Face Ball Game is a simple betting game where players choose face values and place bets. The game uses 3 balls, and each ball randomly lands on a face value.

Players can bet on the following:
	• 9
	• J
	• Q
	• K
	• A

Game Mechanics
The game uses 3 balls.
Each ball randomly selects a value from the available faces (9, J, Q, K, A).
Balls are dropped with an animation effect (visual falling/drawing).
🔹 Betting System
🔹 Betting Patterns (6 total combinations)

Players can bet on different patterns such as:

Specific face value (e.g., all balls = Q)
Combination patterns (depending on your implementation)
🔹 Winning Conditions

The results are based on how many balls match the player’s chosen face:

Single (1 match) → Player wins (low payout)
Double (2 matches) → Player wins (medium payout)
Triple (3 matches) → Player wins (high payout)
🔹 User Options
The player can choose:
Their desired face value
Their bet amount
The number of balls to use (e.g., 1–3 balls)
🔹 Payout Calculation

The payout is calculated by:

Winning Amount = Bet × Multiplier

Multipliers depend on:
Single match → small multiplier
Double match → higher multiplier
Triple match → highest multiplier
🔹 Animation Feature
Balls are displayed with a dropping animation
Each ball reveals its result after landing
Enhances user experience and randomness feel
🔹 Example Flow
User selects:
Face: K
Bet: 100
Balls: 3
Balls roll:
Ball 1 → K
Ball 2 → Q
Ball 3 → K
Result:
2 matches → Double Win
Payout:
100 × (Double Multiplier)
